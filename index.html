Carbon Cash

CarbonCash is Flutter app that aims to encourage people to choose more eco-friendly transportation methods by calculating the money they could save by not driving. In addition, CarbonCash is also capable of determining your ecological footprint in the form of CO2 emissions produced while driving. With the money that is saved by choosing not to drive, it is our goal that users will donate all or some of it to charity. To make this step seamless for users, CarbonCash provides direct links to some of our favourite charitable organizations including TreeCanada, Arbor Day Foundation, and Water.org.
